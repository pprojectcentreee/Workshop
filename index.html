About The Workshop

Ellectra IoT Workshop is a hands-on industrial training program focused on ESP32, embedded systems, and real-time IoT applications aligned with Industry 4.0.

Participants will be introduced to IoT architecture, sensor integration, microcontroller programming, cloud connectivity, and real-time data monitoring.

Why This Workshop?

Practical exposure to hardware interfacing, wireless communication, cloud connectivity, and industrial safety standards.
